Evaluation & Reflection

Zelda Lee
2023-05-06

Evaluation

Consistency

  1. Internal consistency – coherent user interface design and behaving
  2. Metaphorical consistency– similar design with real-world entity

Freedom

  1. Provide clearly marked “emergency exits” to leave unwanted states.
  2. Support “cancel” and “reschedule” functionalities

Flexibility

Allow user to have multiple operations in the same panel

Visibility

  1. Keep users informed of daily dietary intake
  2. Feedback is timely and concrete

Error prevention

  1. Confirm with the user whether the analysis result is correct
  2. Prevent errors from occurring

Reflection

It is important to understand user (people with chronic conditions) needs:

  1. Connecting with healthcare professionals
  2. Tracking their symptoms
  3. Accessing relevant information
  4. Communicating with other patients, etc.

Practice of human-centered design:

  1. Need finding
  2. Ideation
  3. Implementation

Team collaboration:

  1. Organizing tasks
  2. Sharing experience & information
